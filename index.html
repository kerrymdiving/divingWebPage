Discover SCUBA Experience

This is an amazing experience to give you a taster of what being a SCUBA diver can offer you. After a short classroom session, you will be straight into shallow water and experiencing how much fun it is to be able to safely breath underwater! Once comfortable, I can take you exploring the colourful and beautiful coral reef, where you can check out the local wildlife!

Course Prerequisites:
Student: 10 years minimum age. Able to swim and comfortable in the water. Medically fit for diving.
Time Commitment: Classroom session, shallow-water practice, and 40' open-water dive.
Price: $135 US/USD + tax

Specialty Courses

How would you like to try a Night Dive, or a Deep Dive, or even learn some Search and Recovery skills? Would you like to learn how to safely dive with Nitrox? As PADI MAster Instructor, I can offer a large range of Specialty dive courses to give you a taster of all that SCUBA can offer.

Course Prerequisites:
Student: Varies per Specialty
Time Commitment: Varies per Specialty
Price: Varies US/USD + tax

PADI Open Water

This is the first Scuba Instruction level. I’ll be able to safely take you through all elements of the course ensuring you ultimately end up with the skills and knowledge to dive at home or abroad and be an ambassador for the underwater world.

Course Prerequisites:
Student: 10 years minimum age. Able to swim and comfortable in the water. Medically fit for diving.
Time Commitment: PADI Open Water Diver eLearning + 5 confined-water dives + 4 open-water dives
Price: $415 US/USD + tax
